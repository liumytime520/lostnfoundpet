Help you find your LOST pets

It’s okay. Take a deep breath. We know how scary it feels when your pet goes missing. You’re not alone—many people have been in the same place, and help is here. This page will gently guide you through the steps you can take right now to bring your pet home. You don’t need to do everything at once. Just start with the first step—we’re here with you.

Tips for Attracting Public Attention

1. Print your poster and put it in a letter that says Safe Me in bold, then place it in all your neighbours' mailboxes.

2. Go knock on neighbours' doors to ask if they have seen your pet.

3. Post your pet’s poster and message on every media platform you can find.

4. Put multiple clear pictures of your pet on the poster.

5. Offer a monetary reward if possible.

6. Make sure formal organisations (like shelters and councils) are aware of your situation.

7. Put your poster at busy places like bus stop, supermarket doors, schools, parks.

Safety Advice

1. Don’t go alone.Bring a friend or family member.

2. Stay in public areas. Tell someone where you’re going and keep your phone charged.

3.Be careful in hidden or risky places (bushes, forests, holes, under houses). Make sure you can get out, and have someone outside watching or waiting for you.
